The Las Vegas Nevada Temple

This weeks featured temple is the Las Vegas Nevada temple. This magnificent temple was dedicated on December 16, 1989. It remains one of the most beautiful structures in the Las Vegas valley. The gorgeous landscape and building details stand out among the desolate desert. This is symbolic. We highly recommend visiting this temple and our hotel there.
